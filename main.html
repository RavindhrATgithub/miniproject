Student feedback system



Q1. Teacher comes to the class in time




Q2. Teacher comes well prepared in the subject




Q3. Aims/Objectives of the lessons are made clear


Q4. The lessons is organised and presented clearly




Q5. Teacher plans and prepares course materials and additional informations




Q6. Teacher speaks clearly and Audibly




Q7. Teacher writes and draws legibly




Q8. Teacher provides examples of concepts / principles / Explanations are clear and effective




Q9. Teacher's pace and level of instructions are suited to the needs of the students




Q10. Teacher offers assistance and counselling to the needy students




Q11. Teacher asks Question to promote interaction and reflective thinking




Q12. Teacher encourages questioning/ raising doubts by students and answer them well




Q13. Teacher ensures learner activity and problems solving ability in the class




Q14. Teacher encourages, complements and praises originality and creativity displayed by the students




Q15. Teacher gives interesting assignments and that motivate students to seek knowledge and sharpen skills




Q16. Teacher enhances classes regularly and maintains discipline




Q17. Teacher covers the syllabus completely and at an appropiate pace




Q18. Teacher holds tests regularly which are helpful to students in building up confidence in their acquistion and application of knowledge




Q19. Teacher's marking of answer scripts is fair and impartial




Q20. Teacher is prompt in valuing and returing the answer scripts provising feedback on performance
